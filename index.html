About Me

My name is Diana Wallace and I am an Illustrator and Front End Web Developer candidate skilled in writing, clean, testable code. Experienced with mobile-first responsive development with HTML5, CSS3, JQuery, and JavaScript.

With my roots in illustration and art history, I feel that I bring a unique perspective to web & app development and I am looking for a position where I can utilize my skills in art, design, and technology. I am currently based in Denver, Colorado.

I am a dedicated, enthusiastic, and highly motivated illustrator and web developer with experience building web sites and creating illustrations. Formally educated in fine art and illustration I also use Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. I have a passion for art, design, and technology and enjoy blending these three mediums along with modern web technologies.

This site was built with cloud9 ide and bootstrap, which is an open source JavaScript framework. The source code is available on Github.
